Sloppy Joe Recipe

About the Sloppy Joe

The Sloppy Joe is a sandwich consisting of ground beef and onions in a tomato-based sauce served on a hamburger bun. The sandwich likely originated in Sioux City, Iowa in the 1930s. According to legend, the "loose meat sandwich" was the creation of a cook named Joe.

Making the Sloppy Joe is not as sloppy as you think. It takes 5 minutes to prepare, 30 minutes to cook, and you can serve 6 sandwiches!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Onions
  • Green Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the beef, onion, and green pepper.
  2. Drain the liquids, then stir in the remaining ingredients.
  3. Simmer for about 30 minutes. Serve on hamburger buns.
